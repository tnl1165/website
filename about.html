    • Hello! My name is Tiffany Liew.

    I am an aspiring product designer with an interest in graphic design and web development. I just graduated from the University of British Columbia in Vancouver, Canada, with a combined major of Chemistry and Biology, and a minor in Psychology. Throughout my degree, I had always enjoyed the technical/analytical side of science and the observational background of behaviour and mind behind psychology but I felt like I lacked any creative outlet.

    With UX/UI design, I feel like I am able to achieve the perfect combination. Great products come from thorough background research, proper surveying and feedback analysis, and exceptional visual narratives. With graphic design and artistry, I strive for pure expression, innovation and aesthetics in everything that I accomplish.

    On my downtime I enjoy knitting sweaters, taking care of my plants and thrifting as many things as I can.
